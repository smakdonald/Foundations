Exploration 1.40.

Let \(P, Q, A\) and \(B\) be statements. Show that the following are true.
  1. \(P ⇔ P \vee (P \wedge Q)\text{.}\)
  2. \(P ⇔ P \wedge (P \vee Q)\text{.}\)
  3. \(P ↔ Q ⇔ (P \to Q) \wedge (¬P \to ¬Q)\text{.}\)
  4. \(P \to (A \wedge B) ⇔ (P \to A) \wedge (P \to B)\text{.}\)
  5. \(P \to (A \vee B) ⇔ (P \wedge ¬A) \to B\text{.}\)
  6. \((A \vee B) \to Q ⇔ (A \to Q) \wedge (B \to Q)\text{.}\)
  7. \((A \wedge B) \to Q ⇔ (A \to Q) \vee (B \to Q)\text{.}\)
  8. \((A \wedge B) \to Q ⇔ A \to (B \to Q)\text{.}\)
