Theorem 8.43.

Let A be a set, and let u : A \times A \to A and t : A \times A \to A be binary operations on A. Suppose that u and t satisfy the following properties. Let x, y, z \in A.
  1. x u y = y u x and x t y = y t x.
  2. x u (y u z) = (x u y) u z and x t (y t z) = (x t y) t z.
  3. x u (x t y) = x and x t (x u y) = x.
Let 4 be the relation on A defined by x 4 y if and only if x u y = x, for all x, y \in A. Then (A, 4) is a lattice, with u and t the meet and join of the lattice, respectively.
