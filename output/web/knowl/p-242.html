⇐. Suppose that \(a = b\) or \(a = -b\text{.}\) First, suppose that \(a = b\text{.}\) Then \(a · 1 = b\text{,}\) so \(a|b\text{,}\) and \(b · 1 = a\text{,}\) so \(b|a\text{.}\) Similarly, suppose that \(a = -b\text{.}\) Then \(a · (-1) = b\text{,}\) so \(a|b\text{,}\) and \(b · (-1) = a\text{,}\) so \(b|a\text{.}\)
